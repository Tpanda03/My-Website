*** APPLE AND ITS CULTURAL INFLUENCE IN THE 90s ***
Research Paper Summary
By Micah Manookian - 11/05/2025
Key Quotes from the Research

Introduction Micah Manookian - 11/05/2025
The 1990s were such a defining decade in the rise of the internet as we know it and the personal computing culture. While many companies like Microsoft and AOL have helped shape the technical foundation of the early fragmented web; Apple would influence the way people creatively could connect with it.
Steve Jobs Quote 1998 iMac Launch
"It will set the new gold standard and it is beautiful" - Steve Jobs, 1998
iMac Sales Performance From WIRED Article (1998)
"iMac's rankings have steadily improved, moving from fourth place in August, to third in September and second in October."
Paolo Magaudda Quote Academic Research
"The fascination that Apple's objects have exerted on contemporary society is the outcome of the interaction between various elements that are simultaneously technological and cultural, symbolic and material."
Conclusion Micah Manookian - 11/05/2025
Apple's influence on the 1990s internet culture was not defined solely by its innovative hardware or software, but rather its personal redefinition of what technology could represent itself. By connecting computing to human creativity, self-expression, and human-centered design; this led Apple to really reshape the public attitudes towards tech.
